These are not ``functions``; rather, they are __relations__.

For example, a statement of the kind:

<pre class='mcdp_statements'>
    a >= sqrt(b)
</pre>

In fact, it is different than:

<pre class='mcdp_statements'>
    sqrt(a) >= b
</pre>
